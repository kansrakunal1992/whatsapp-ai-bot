Frequently Asked Questions

Is my business information secure?

Yes, we use bank-grade encryption to protect all your business data. Your information is only used to provide you with better service and is never shared with third parties without your consent.

Can I change these details later?

Absolutely! You can update your business information at any time through your account settings. Changes to your WhatsApp number may require verification.

What if I don't have a WhatsApp Business number?

You can use a regular WhatsApp number to get started. However, we recommend upgrading to WhatsApp Business for additional features like business profile, quick replies, and labels.

How will this information be used?

We use your business details to:

  • Personalize your automated responses
  • Set up your business profile
  • Optimize response times during your operating hours
  • Provide location-specific services if enabled
Can I get support in my local language?

Yes! Based on the language you select, our support team will communicate with you in your preferred language where possible. The AI responses will also be generated in your selected language.
