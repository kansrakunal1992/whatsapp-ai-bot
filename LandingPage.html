How does the AI WhatsApp bot work?

Our AI WhatsApp bot integrates seamlessly with your WhatsApp Business account to automatically respond to customer inquiries. It uses natural language processing to understand questions and provide relevant responses, just like a human would.

The bot can be trained on your specific products/services and learns from every interaction to improve its responses over time.

What setup is required?

Setting up is simple:

  1. Connect your WhatsApp Business account
  2. Upload your FAQ or product information
  3. Customize responses to match your brand voice
  4. Go live in minutes

No technical knowledge is required - we guide you through every step.

Can I customize the responses?

Yes! You have full control over the bot's responses. You can:

  • Set predefined answers for common questions
  • Adjust the tone to match your brand personality
  • Create custom workflows for complex inquiries
  • Add your own business-specific responses
What languages does it support?

  • Common question trends
